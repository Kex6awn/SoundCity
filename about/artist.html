About SoundCity Artists

SoundCity is committed to promoting emerging and established talent across South Dakota and the global music scene. Our platform helps artists grow their fanbase, gain exposure, and connect with listeners through powerful streaming tools and media coverage.

Through exclusive interviews, live events, curated playlists, and artist showcases, SoundCity bridges the gap between musicians and their audiences. Whether you're an indie artist or a chart-topper, our mission is to amplify your voice and elevate your sound without a record label.

Join artists like Burna Boy, Kexhawn, Nasty_CSA, and more who trust SoundCity as their music discovery and promotion platform.
